Home Details

I am originally from Alappuzha, a beautiful district in the state of Kerala, India. Known as the 'Venice of the East', Alappuzha is famous for its serene backwaters, intricate network of canals, and lush greenery. The backwaters are a defining feature of the region, where life revolves around the water, from transportation to agriculture.

The picture on the left captures the tranquility of the backwaters, with a traditional houseboat gracefully gliding through the calm waters, surrounded by palm trees. This is a place that truly feels like home.
